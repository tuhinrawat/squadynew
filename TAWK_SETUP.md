# Tawk.to Chat Widget Setup

## Changing the Widget Title to "Auction Chat"

The chat widget title ("Customer Support") needs to be changed in your Tawk.to dashboard. Here's how:

### Steps to Change Title:

1. **Log in to Tawk.to**
   - Go to https://dashboard.tawk.to/
   - Log in with your account

2. **Navigate to Widget Settings**
   - Click on your property (or select it from the dropdown)
   - Go to **Administration** (gear icon in top menu)
   - Under **Channels**, select **Chat Widget**

3. **Edit Widget Content**
   - Scroll down to **Widget Content** section
   - Click **Edit Content**

4. **Modify the Header**
   - Under **Header Cards**, find the **Heading** card
   - Click the pencil icon to edit
   - Change the text from "Customer Support" to **"Auction Chat"**
   - Click **Update** to save

5. **Save Changes**
   - Click the X or press ESC to exit the editor
   - The widget will automatically update on your website
